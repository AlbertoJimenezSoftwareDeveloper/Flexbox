Arabian horse

The Arabian is an ancient, highly intelligent, and spirited breed recognized by its refined, wedge-shaped head, dished profile, high tail carriage, and large eyes. Standing roughly 14.1–15.1 hands, these compact, agile horses possess incredible stamina and are known for their loyalty to humans. Common colors include gray, chestnut, bay, and black.
